User Group

  • Who: My Grandmother
  • Age: 89 years old
  • Ethnicity: Chinese
  • Location: Lives in Beijing, China
  • User type: A recent adapter of smartphones

User Research

How is the user using her smartphone?

  • Uses an Android phone
  • Uses wechat to communication with family members and friends, to obtain updates about local dinning hall and to read the news.
  • Video Streaming and mini mobile games
  • Uses smartphone for various payments (electricity, water, gas, groceries).

Pain Points

    Observation Findings

  • Struggles with small text (chinese characters)
  • Workflows are hard to be remembered
  • Nervous when pressing buttons, too much force is applied
  • Frustrated when the outcome isn’t what she expected and would tend to give up
  • Icons' significations are not intuitive

    Interview Findings

  • Sometimes would accidentally delete apps
  • Multiple payment apps, too many to remember and manage
  • Steps for payments are often complex and confusing
  • Worried that personal info and bank account info entered in apps would be stolen
  • For each payment, entry of 18 digits ID number and bank account and password are required
  • Pressing buttons on screen does not give a clear feedback
  • Difficult for elders to get taxis

    Solution: Centralized payments

  • An app with simple and short workflows for payments
  • Available for paying bills or groceries shopping
  • Won’t require re-entry of personal information for every payment
  • Clear feedback for buttons pressed
  • Workflows should be clear and intuitive

Hero's Journey



Personas


Olmoonee, 78 years-old

Frustrations: unable to use popular online payment apps for her utilities’ payments. Feels left out of current technology trends.

Goals: fully embrace online payment and reinforce relationship with her grandson Tommy.


Charles, 84 years-old

Frustrations: all kinds of payments are moving online and Charles finds it hard to adjust to the change.

Goals:find an easy to use app that would help him transition from traditional payments to online payments.



Rebranding

The final decision for the app name then became: Silver Wallet
