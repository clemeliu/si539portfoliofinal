Storyboard

Problem

Olmoonee struggles to use online payment apps like Wechat Pay or Alipay. They are too complex and she is frustrated.


She feels that there is a huge gap between her and her grandson as she can’t reach the technology world of his grandson.


Olmoonee is also afraid of entering bank info repeatedly online. It is tedious and unsafe as no one is assuring her private info’s safety.





Solution

Her grandson suggests her to tryout the app SilverWallet. Olmoonee downloads it


Skeptical, she tries to use the app to pay her water bills.


Olmoonee is surprised by how easy the whole payment process was. Her payment info are also saved for her next payments.





Benefits

Olmoonee sends some gratitude money to her grandson in few easy steps.


Olmoonee deepens her bond with her grandson now that she finally understands online payment.


Olmoonee is thrilled that she can perform all kinds of monetary tasks with just a few clicks on her phone.
